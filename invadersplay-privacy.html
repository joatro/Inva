Privacy Policy for InvadersPlay

Effective Date: July 25, 2024

We, [joetro], take your privacy very seriously. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it in our application, "InvadersPlay".

1. Data We Collect

Our application is designed to collect the least amount of personal information possible. However, we use third-party services that may collect data to function correctly:

  • Usage Data and Analytics (via Google Analytics for Firebase): We collect anonymous information about how you play, such as session duration, features used, and errors that may occur. This helps us understand how to improve the game for you. This information does not personally identify you.
  • Advertising Data (via Google AdMob): To show relevant ads in the free version of the game, AdMob may collect and use data such as your device's advertising identifier and your IP address. This allows ads to be shown that may be of interest to you. If you purchase the ad-free version, this data collection for advertising stops.
  • Purchase Data (via Google Play Billing): If you decide to purchase the option to remove ads, the payment is processed securely by Google Play. We do not receive or store any of your credit card or bank account information. We only receive confirmation of the purchase from Google.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

  • Operate and improve the game: Fix bugs and understand which features are most popular.
  • Monetize the application: Show ads in the free version to fund development.
  • Manage your purchases: Unlock the ad-free experience if you have purchased it.

3. Third Party Services

Our application relies on the following services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Analytics para Firebase

We recommend you read their policies to understand how they manage your data.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the collected information and ensure it is handled securely.
